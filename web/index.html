How it works

Four layers. One tap to act.

1
Fetch
Every 30 minutes, RWAI pulls live yield data for USDY and mETH from DeFiLlama and compares it to the last snapshot.
2
Explain
When yield shifts more than 0.1% or the spread crosses 1.5%, Claude Sonnet writes a plain-English explanation — what changed, why it happened, and what it means for your position size specifically.
3
Propose
The agent sends a Telegram message with an Approve / Dismiss inline keyboard. Nothing executes until you tap Approve.
4
Anchor
Every approved decision is logged on Mantle Mainnet via the ERC-8004 Reputation Registry — a verifiable, auditable AI track record that accumulates from Day 1.
