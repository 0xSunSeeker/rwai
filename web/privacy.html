Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

RWAI is designed with privacy as a default. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service and nothing more. We do not sell, share, or monetise any user data.

2. What We Collect

When you connect your wallet to rwai.fyi, we read your public wallet address and your on-chain token balances for USDY, mETH, and cmETH on Mantle Mainnet. This data is already publicly visible on the blockchain — we are simply reading it to display your positions.


We do not collect your name, email address, IP address, or any personally identifiable information.


If you use the Telegram bot, your Telegram user ID is stored locally on the server running the RWAI agent to associate your preferences (delegation tier, alert settings) with your session. This data is not shared with any third party.

3. On-Chain Data

When RWAI logs a decision to the Mantle Mainnet via ERC-8004, the following data is written permanently to the blockchain: a hash of the agent's reasoning, a timestamp, and the agent's wallet address. Your wallet address is only written on-chain when you explicitly approve a transaction.


Blockchain data is public and permanent by nature. Once written on-chain, it cannot be deleted.

4. Cookies and Tracking

rwai.fyi uses no advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking tools. The only data stored in your browser is your wallet connection state, which is cleared when you disconnect.

5. Third-Party Services

RWAI fetches yield data from DeFiLlama (defillama.com) and token prices from CoinGecko (coingecko.com). These requests are made from your browser directly. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle request data.

6. Data Retention

Telegram bot preferences are stored locally on the RWAI server and can be deleted upon request. On-chain data cannot be deleted as it is permanent by design. We do not maintain any other databases of user information.

7. Your Rights

You can disconnect your wallet at any time, which removes your session data from our frontend. You can request deletion of any off-chain data we hold about you by contacting us.

8. Children

RWAI is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. We will note the date of the last update at the top of this page.
